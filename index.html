NUSAMUDA COFFEE

PT. Sekawan Muda Nusantara has been ratified by the Minister of Law and Human Rights of the Republic of Indonesia since 2022 and has a sector in coffee named Nusamuda Coffee, which is focusing on coffee beans and roasted coffee beans. PT. Sekawan Muda Nusantara has a base location in Jember, East Java, Indonesia.

Our company has experience in processing coffee beans and has spread across East Java, Indonesia. Our company also maintains the quality of coffee beans by optimizing protocols of cultivation, ripe berries collection, and removal of the outer fruit layers by dry or wet processes and moisture reduction. These coffee beans are from the plantation on the foothill Ijen-Raung mountains.

Ijen Raung Coffee

The Ijen-Raung mountain area has an altitude between 900-1,500 meters above sea level. Types of coffee cultivated include Arabica coffee and Robusta coffee. Ijen-Raung Coffee has a distinctive taste, and it is favored by the world coffee market. This distinctive taste makes Ijen Raung Arabica Coffee get a geographical indication certificate from the Ministry of Law and Human Rights of the Republic of Indonesia as Java Ijen-Raung Coffee.
